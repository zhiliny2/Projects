Model1 has a smaller MAPE, but model2 has a smaller MSE.

  • If you prioritize the accuracy of predictions relative to the actual values and aim to minimize the percentage error, Model1, which has a smaller MAPE, would be the preferred choice.

  • Conversely, if the magnitude of the errors is of utmost importance and your goal is to minimize the squared differences between the predicted and actual values, then Model2, which has a smaller MSE, would be more suitable.
