Question 2

What is the appropriate Holt-Winters method for this data set (multiplicative / additive)? why?

As we can see from the plot in Question1, the sesonal variations are not constant through the series. In addition, the amplitude of the seasonal pattern is proportional to the average level within the season.

Therefore, the appropriate Holt-Winters method for this data set should be the multiplicative method.
